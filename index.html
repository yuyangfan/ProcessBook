Introduction

This process book goes through all the steps that our group have taken to describe my team’s solution to the final design of UW Textbook Market, and it details the process behind our solution. The design problem my team derived at the beginning of the class is, “How can we help University of Washington students to buy or sell textbook at their target price?” From the beginning of our loose design space and idea exploration, to the final design of our fully functional textbook trading website, this process book includes sketches, design tests and new ideas we come up with during the implementation of UW Textbook Market. Although this project is done by team including Brandon Thai, Ria Hu and myself, this book mainly focuses on my personal experiences and thoughts.
